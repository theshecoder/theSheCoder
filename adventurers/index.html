< LET'S BE ADVENTURERS DARLING >

| 7.2.2015 | Photography | UTAH Adventure

Oh how excited I am! Summer has arrived + adventure tme is officially here! Living in Utah, there are always so many new adventures to experience and I'm here to start #theUltimateSummerBucketList. First on the list: Snowbird Aerial Tram Ride

Meet Jake. Jake is my #ultimateAdventurerPartner. He's simply the best. Together we rode the tram Saturday evening + fell so in love that we went back and did it again Monday night! I promise you, it is totally worth the $17 tram ride. It is honestly one of the coolest things I've ever experienced! Trams leave every 30 minutes on the hour + half hour mark. The last tram goes up at 8:00 + arrives back at the base at 8:30. This is the ride I would suggest as the sun is beginning to set + it is absolutely B E A U T I F U L. Don't forget your camera the first time like we did, you'll totally regret it!
Have fun my little adventurers!
