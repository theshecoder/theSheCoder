Hello my darlings. Welcome, welcome! My name is Amanda Jo. I'm a 21 year old programmer born to design, create and engineer. Whether it was beating my dad at Mario Kart on the Super Nintendo, running the football into the end zone during recess, or defeating my fellow nerds at the Mathletes competition, I always knew I could keep up with the boys. Since a young age, I have been the girl to break the mold. Now majoring in computer science at BYU, I’m ready to show everyone that ‘girls rule and boys drool’.

As "Hello World" is the first program any coder writes, I thought it was only fitting to caption my first blog post just that. I live to merge the technical and design worlds and hope to begin doing so with this blog. My first project of the summer, well you're looking at it! TheSheCoder has been built and deisgned by me--from scratch. Using HTML, JavaScript + CSS, I was able to create my website. Here you will find fashion finds, technology tips + adventure suggestions.

I created this blog to document my life. My adventures. My dreams. My style. My goals. My creations.

Enjoy!
