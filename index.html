< CAMO + CREATIVE CLOUDS >

| 8.22.2015 |

Where to even begin? This past week has been quite the adjustment! With not having work everyday and some changes in my personal life, I found myself with a LOT more free time! Luckily this meant more time to design, learn + work on my portfolio. The website has some new adjustments I'm super excited about! The first being the lovely new Portfolio Tab. I'm excited for new opportunities to learn + explore and will be sharing all my work there if anyone is interested in checking them out!
The next update I've been working on is my Previous Posts Page link at the bottom of my home page. I'm super excited to get this working. It has been quite tricky to implement because my entire site is hard coded. (For anyone who doesn't enjoy hearing code talk, skip to the next paragraph now!) This means I don't really have a database or anything to hold all of my posts. Luckily I figured out some super nifty javaScript to create div tags for each of my prior pages. Long story short, now I am able to show all of my posts in one nifty place and the site is much easier to navigate!
In addition to the new site add-ons, I also gained some great new freelance jobs designing for some start-up companies and creating a really great website! I'm super excited to get to work and am having too much fun thinking of all the options!

Last but definitely not least, I scored a position as an Adobe Student Rep for BYU campus. I cannot tell you how excited I am! Adobe has always been my dream and working with the Adobe Students Team is definitely a necessary stepping stone. I will host workshops on campus about the Creative Cloud software which I could not be more excited about. I also hear they are going to hook me up with some sweet swagger, so obviously I'm pumped! If there are any BYU students reading or following the blog who are curious about the Creative Cloud, send me an email and let's get together! I can't wait for this collaberation! And hey, who knows? Maybe it will lead to an internship or full-time position down the road, eh? ;)

< FALL FLANNELS >

| 8.15.2015 |

Fall semester is just around the corner and I can hardly wait! Can you believe the semester starts in 2 WEEKS?! I've had so much fun getting ready for school. From new tech accessories (will be featured in an upcoming post), new apartment decor, school supplies and school clothes, I am getting more excited by the minute. I've had so much fun transitioning my summer essentials to perfect fall pairings. I paired my white jeans with some of my favorite booties, a simple peach tee and my new flannel to create the perfect "in-between" look! Plus, you can never go wrong with flannel...I'm totally obsessed!
Happy almost Fall and Go Cougars! :)

< DENIM + DESIGN >

| 8.8.2015 |

Well yesterday was officially my last day as a Software Engineer in Test Intern at Instructure Inc. It's bitter sweet knowing that my first internship is over but I am so grateful for the opportunity. With the next three weeks before school starts up again, I hope to stay busy updating the blog, creating some new features, and designing away! I really need to get going on my portfolio and design work so if anyone has any graphic design needs or wants, let this girl know!

This post quickly became one of my all time favorites because I absolutely A D O R E this romper. And can you believe I got it at Old Navy?! On sale! I was so excited because I'm so tall, it's really hard to find rompers that fit me! (lol) Anyway, I love the laid back vibe of the romper dressed up slighly with some red lips and a nice floppy. (I'm obsessed, I know) In addition to shooting this post this week, I also designed my new business card to use at the upcoming STEM fair at BYU next month! I really love the way they turned out and can't wait to see them printed! I wanted to showcase my design skills while still keeping a techinal and professional look and I think the two worlds merged very beautifully!
Onto more adventures, design + code! I can't even wait!
