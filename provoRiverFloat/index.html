Happy 4th of July!

How lucky are we to live in the land of the free + the home of the brave. The 4th of July has always been my absolute favorite holiday + I have literally been counting down the days! I love celebrating our country: the red, white + blue, our freedom + liberty, and all those that fought to keep our country safe + free. I can't wait for the watermelon + fireworks tonight and hope you all have the absolute best holiday!

Stay cool today with a summer dress, paired with white kicks and an old flannel. The perfect ensemble for those independence day celebrations. Paired with some bright red lips and slick black sunnies + you're all set! Happy Birthday 'Merica! And hey, don't forget to eat an extra hot dog for me!
